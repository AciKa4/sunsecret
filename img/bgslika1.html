Specialist bras, swimwear & breast prostheses

Did you know that we can look back on over 45 years of experience in the manufacture of breast prostheses?        ANITA develops and produces its own breast prostheses, mastectomy bras and swimwear.

That is how they can be perfectly coordinated with each other. In addition to these three ranges, you will also find products for post-operative care, like compression bras or lymph drainage bandages that help speed up the healing process after a mastectomy.

All models are thoroughly tested for safety, comfort and come in attractive and fashionable designs.

All our breast care products are thoroughly tested by affected women before they reach the market.

That is how you can be absolutely sure that Anita care products comply with the strictest quality standards.
